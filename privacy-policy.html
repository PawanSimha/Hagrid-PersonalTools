Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

1. Privacy First Philosophy

Hagrid is built on a "Privacy First" principle. We believe your data belongs to you. Unlike traditional online tools, 99% of Hagrid's utilities process your files locally within your web browser. Your images and documents are never uploaded to our servers for processing.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal identification information (PII). We do not use tracking cookies, and we do not sell your data to third parties.

  • Local Processing: Tools like Image Compressor, Format Converter, and PDF tools run entirely in your browser.
  • Server Processing: Tools that use the FastAPI backend for processing (image inversion, pencil sketch, PDF conversion) process files ephemerally in memory. No data is written to disk or retained after the response is sent.

3. Browser Storage

Hagrid may use browser storage (like LocalStorage) solely to remember your theme preference (Dark/Light mode). No project or file data is ever stored persistently in your browser without your action.

4. Third-Party Services

Hagrid uses standard CDNs (like Google Fonts and CDNJS) to load essential assets efficiently. These services may log basic technical metadata (like IP addresses) as part of standard web traffic, but we do not share any user activity with them.

5. International Standards

We strive to maintain high privacy standards consistent with global regulations like GDPR and CCPA, providing you with full control over your data by keeping it on your machine.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Contact Page.
