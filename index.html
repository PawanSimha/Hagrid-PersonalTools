About Hagrid

Hagrid is a simple, personal workspace of lightweight developer utilities — built from scratch by Pawan Simha, an MCA (Master of Computer Science) student. What started as a side project to sharpen web development skills quickly grew into a handy toolkit for everyday image processing tasks.

No ads, no sign-ups, no bloat — just fast, accessible tools that do exactly what they say. Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, because simplicity is the ultimate sophistication.
